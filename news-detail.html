About me and things I do in my free time.

Being born and raised in a developing country, where a few of typical township families are a able to make ends meet. I have witnessed firsthand the disparity between the haves and the have nots right early on in my life. However, I am so greatful for everything that has happened in my life thus far, it has shaped me to be the good person I am today. My late dad always told me to thrive to be a good person by all means. Over the past decade, my academic and professional endeavours are an attempt to lean "my life antique weighing scale" towards the haves and to adapt responsibilities that comes with my little good fortune whenever my "antique weighing life scale" leans in the "haves."

Now, going back to the things I enjoy doing on my spare time. I am an explorer, I like experimenting and trying out things. I enjoy out activities mostly. Being inspired by the great Rodney Mullen, I adapted skateboarding of which I happened to be fairly good in it through continuous practice. I am a semi-frequent long distance runner, I enjoy playing musical instruments. Oops, before I forget, I love coffee by the way.

I recently went for a mountain bike hike with a couple of colleagues, I enjoyed it so much that I am even contemplating of being adapting raod cycling as my new hobby. I love travelling, new places they somehow breathe life in my body.

I also enjoy good food, I am so fortunate to have two sisters who are good in cooking food. I really enjoy the food they prepare.
